Art Projects for Young People

We offer different art projects. Especially for children that live in refugee camps. These projects are about painting, crafting, dancing, or making music.

With our art projects we give the participants a safe place. This way they can develop their creative capabilities. They discover, realise and strengthen their own ressources and abilities. They gain self-esteem and trust in themselves, as well as in others and the staff members of KuB.

Current projects

Currently there is an art project that takes place every Friday from 4 p.m. until 8 p.m. at the Marie-Schlei Haus. It is made in collaboration with AWO Kreisverband Berlin Mitte e.V. and started in May 2022.
