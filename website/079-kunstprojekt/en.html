Call for volunteers for the art project

Last Updated: 7th June 2022

The KuB Art Project for Young People "Communication and Games through different Artistic Languages" is searching for volunteers:

  1. to participate in the project’s sessions on fridays
  2. to do a 1 to 4 times workshop about any kind of art medium/language.

Already formed concepts of a workshop are welcomed as well as an initial idea to build up together with the coordinator of the art project.
