Network "German Courses for All!"

The KuB and ten other initiatives from Berlin offer free German courses for refugees. We founded the network "German Courses for All!" in 2013.

We want:

  • to get an overview of the existing range of free German courses in Berlin.
  • coordinate new consecutive German courses.
  • make better offers to interested students (closer to home, language level, etc.)
  • to offer teachers and students better opportunities to communicate.
  • to create markets for rooms and materials.
  • to support the formation of new German courses.
  • people who want to teach to find students interested in the initiatives.

More information at www.netzwerk-deutschkurse-fuer-alle.de
