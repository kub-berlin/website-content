The Team

The people working at KuB come from different countries. They all bring their own experiences, as do the people who seek advice from us.

Our staff members come from many different professions. For example: lawyers, social workers, family assistants, psychologists, German philologists, art therapists, cultural scientists, social scientists, drama teachers, computer scientists, historians, (male) nurses and students of different study courses.

To those seeking advice we can offer an experienced team with different areas of expertise. We all work closely together. Our staff members work independently on tasks corresponding to their area of interest and expertise. Through regularl further education courses they keep up to date with new regulations and learn new methods in social work.
