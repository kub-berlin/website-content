Counselling for women refugees

Getting an Appointment

If you need a consultation:
Please give us a call: 030 / 614 94 00
Or contact us via e-mail: kontakt@kub-berlin.org

We will give you an appointment or recommend another place.

The specialized consultation service for women focuses on refugee women, women with insecure residence status and women without a residence permit. If you are a citizen of the European Union, we might not be the right place for you.

We provide assistance to women who are in need of special protection. Namely:

  • Pregnant women
  • Single mothers
  • Women affected by domestic violence
  • Women affected by sexualized violence

We give advice on:

  • questions concerning the right of residence, right of asylum and social assistance law
  • psychiatric or social problems

We are also located in the offices of KuB. For our consultation we create a safe and secure atmosphere. Together we work out how you can solve your problems, focusing on what is most important for you.

Our female consultants have lots of experience. They will look at your whole situation and support you accordingly.

We assist you along your path to finding a solution. For example we have people who can accompany you to administrative offices / authorities, doctors and lawyers.

We also have language helpers. They will translate for you from German to your language and vice versa, during a consultation or at the authorities. The interpreters know and understand your situation.

If you feel more comfortable you can ask for a consultation anonymously. You do not have to tell us your name.

KuB is part of the Berlin network for refugees with special need for protection. Within this network KuB is the specialised unit for consultation of women in need. KuB cooperates with BBZ, a consulting and assistance centre for young and minor age refugees.
