Internship at KuB

Great that you are interested in an internship at KuB! Here you can find some information about internships at KuB:

This is what awaits you at KuB

  • Overview of the KuB
  • Working in self-organized structures
  • Insights into asylum and residence law
  • Insights into the topic of refuge and migration

Areas of responsibility

We are organized in different work areas. The focus of your internship should be in at least one work area. It is possible to also work in other areas.

We would like all interns to work in the "office" area. This means

  • Initial contact with addressees
  • low-threshold contact point
  • „Clearing“/Clarify requests
  • Identify adequate further counseling centers or referral possibilities and provide addressees with information about them.

Depending on interests and needs, cooperation in other areas of the KuB is also possible and desirable. E.g.:

  • Legal consultation (asylum and residence law)
  • Art project
  • Administration (project management, club management, etc.)
  • Language help and accompaniment
  • Public Relations

Requirements

  • We expect the internship to last at least 3 months, but are very happy to receive longer internships
  • Willingness to confront forms of discrimination (especially racism)
  • High degree of willingness to work independently

Miscellaneous

Part-time internships are possible with us. We try to take your living conditions into account during the internship.

We especially welcome applications from Black people, People of Color, and people with a history of refuge and/or migration.

Many universities have recognized us as an internship site. A compulsory internship (before or during studies) for social work programs is possible with us. Guidance by social workers/social pedagogues is provided.

Also feel free to contact us if you want to intern with us in a different setting.

Unfortunately, we cannot pay for internships.

If you have further questions, please feel free to contact us.
